Welcome to Igor & Lidija's RUSSIA

Ongoing seminars on Igor Mel'čuk and Lidija Iordanskaja's (almost) 2 centuries' worth of knowledge.

We’re Back!

Initiated during the 2020 pandemic, my grandparents' virtual seminars are now resuming where they left off in 2021. The bi-weekly lectures will alternate between discussions on Russian poets and topics in linguistics. For those of you who do not know Igor, he is unbelievably eager to impart his decades’ worth of knowledge to you.

The Art of Storytelling

Although not a historian by training, Igor Mel'čuk is a world-class linguist who also just so happens to be a world-class story teller. His wife and lifelong colleague – Lidija Iordanskaja – will be diligently by his side to ensure that he does not stave too far from history into the realm of myth.
