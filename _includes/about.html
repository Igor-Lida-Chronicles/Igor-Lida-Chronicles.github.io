Welcome to Igor & Lidija's RUSSIA

Ongoing seminars on Igor Mel'čuk and Lidija Iordanskaja's (almost) 2 centuries' worth of knowledge.

We’re Back!

Initiated during the 2020 pandemic, my grandparents' virtual seminars are now resuming where they left off in 2021. The bi-weekly lectures will alternate between discussions on Russian poets and topics in linguistics. For those of you who do not know Igor, he is unbelievably eager to impart his decades’ worth of knowledge to you.

The Art of Storytelling

Although not a historian by training, this world-class linguist also just so happens to be a world-class story teller. His wife and lifelong colleague – Lidija Iordanskaja – will be diligently by his side to ensure that he does not stave too far from history into the realm of myth.

Linguistics

The history lectures will cover a span of 1000 years, including tsars, Decembrists, Bolsheviks, and notable poets.
Recognizing Russia’s deep connection with poetry, separate poetry lectures will explore each poet's biography and selected works.
Additionally, once a month, we will hold a linguistics seminar where my grandparents will share their life's work in the field.
